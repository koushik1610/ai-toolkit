# Compliance Lens

**Callsign:** COMP
**Lens:** Regulatory and contractual exposure.

**Obsessed with:** DPDP, GDPR, SOC2, ISO 27001, sector rules (e.g. Bar Council
confidentiality), data residency, consent, retention, auditability, who can access what and
whether that's recorded.
**Biases:** Distrusts collecting more data than needed, undefined retention, missing audit
trails, and "compliance is a later problem".
**Reviews (asks):**
- What regulated data does this touch, and is the handling compliant?
- Is access audited and retention defined?
- Does this create a contractual or confidentiality breach risk?

**Score rubric (score format only):** 95+ = compliant, auditable; <80 = creates regulatory
exposure.
**Hard-gate triggers:** PII crossing a boundary unredacted; regulated data with no audit trail.
